Power & Early Setup

Rounds 1–5
STEP 01
Activate all three power generators
Three generators are scattered across the island. Generator A is in the docks area, Generator B is near the main prison block, and Generator C is in the back jungle path. Activating all three unlocks the Pack-a-Punch.
Image coming soon
Required Unlocks PaP
  1. Generator A — Docks: Head down from spawn toward the water. The generator is inside the Shipping Office building, ground floor, east wall.
  2. Generator B — Prison Block: Take the main corridor past the cafeteria. The generator room is behind a breakable wall — melee it once to open.
  3. Generator C — Research Lab: Go through the jungle tunnel, past the waterfall. The generator is on the upper platform of the Bio-Research Building.

⚠ Each generator spawns 2 Manglers when activated. Have your weapon Pack-a-Punched before activating B and C.

Keres — The Terminus Sentinel

A mutated bio-weapon with three distinct phases. Weak to the Beamsmasher and explosive damage.

1

Phase 1 — Claw Rush (100% → 66% HP)

Keres charges at players in a straight line. Always sprint left or right — never backwards. Exposed back weakpoint glows orange. Hit it with Beamsmasher charged shots for 3× damage. Adds: Standard zombie waves.

2

Phase 2 — Shield Wall (66% → 33% HP)

Keres extends a bio-electric shield. The shield blocks all conventional damage. Shoot the 4 glowing orbs orbiting its body (use Beamsmasher EMP alt-fire to chain them). Once all 4 orbs are destroyed the shield drops for 8 seconds — unload everything. Adds: Manglers + Disciples.

3

Phase 3 — Enrage (33% → 0% HP)

Keres increases speed and spawns clones. The real Keres has a fully red health bar (clones show 40% bars). Focus fire on the real one. Beamsmasher chains will arc from real → clones, helping clear. Adds: Continuous Amalgam spawns.

Boss Fight Survival Tips

  • Never stop moving. Keres locks on if you stand still for > 2 seconds.
  • Keep Quick Revive active — it will proc at least once during Phase 3.
  • Use Brain Rot ammo to convert a zombie as a decoy during the shield phase.
  • In squads, assign one player as "orb caller" — they call out which orb is next.
  • There is no timer on the fight — take your time and stay calm in Phase 3.
