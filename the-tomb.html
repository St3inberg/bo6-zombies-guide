Apothicon Servant Boss Fight

Rounds 11+

After building the Apothicon Sword, the boss fight begins. The Apothicon Servant is a powerful enemy that requires coordinated attacks.

1

Phase 1 — Initial Attack

Use the Apothicon Sword to damage the Servant. Focus on its weak points.

2

Phase 2 — Shield Break

Break through the Servant's shields using charged attacks.

3

Phase 3 — Final Strike

Deliver the final blow to defeat the Apothicon Servant.

🏛 Boss Survival Tips

  • Stay mobile and avoid the Servant's powerful attacks.
  • Use the Apothicon Sword's special abilities effectively.
  • Coordinate with your team for maximum damage output.
